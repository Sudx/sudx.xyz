The Code is Free, but its Developers Are Not.

Free and open-source software runs the world. From the servers that power the internet to the Linux ecosystem that champions freedom, it's all built by a global community. However, its creators—the developers, maintainers, and designers—often rely on unstable donations, volunteer work, and fragmented funding.

Community gratitude exists, but it rarely converts into fair and sustainable financial support. SUDX was born to fix this, creating a direct and transparent bridge between the open-source ethos and the funding power of Web3.

Turning Gratitude into Action.

In the Linux terminal, sudo is the command that grants elevated privileges to execute critical tasks. It embodies trust, power, and responsibility. Our currency, SUDX, is born from this philosophy to become the native token of the open-source ecosystem. The X stands for extension, experimentation, and the future we will build together.

Native Integration

Our vision is to integrate SUDX directly into the tools developers live in: Git, CI/CD pipelines, code editors, and decentralized networks.

A Truly Autonomous DAO, Powered by Aragon.

Our governance model is designed for maximum security and true decentralization, eliminating central points of failure. We achieve this through a robust architecture powered by Aragon.

Automated Execution

When the community approves a proposal, the DAO's smart contracts execute it automatically. No manual intervention is needed from the team, ensuring the community's will is law.

Two-Layer Treasury

For security, funds are split between a smaller Operational Treasury for daily use (managed by the DAO) and a large Strategic Treasury (in a Safe), which only refills the DAO's budget after a community vote.

Tokenomics: Fair and Transparent

A clear and fair distribution is the foundation of trust. Our tokenomics are designed for long-term growth and to empower the community.
Total Supply: 2,800,000,000 SUDX

Ecosystem & Rewards

45% (1.26B SUDX)
To fund open-source projects, reward contributors, and grow the ecosystem through community governance (DAO).

Gradual Supply Release

To ensure market stability, the total supply is not released at once. Major allocations like Team and Ecosystem funds are locked and vest over several years, ensuring a slow and predictable increase in circulating supply.

Liquidity Pool

~24.95% (~698.5M SUDX)
This is the total budget for market liquidity. Only a small fraction will be used to create the initial SUDX/USDC pool, which will be locked. The rest will be added gradually via DAO governance to ensure long-term market stability.

Market Launch Strategy

Scenario A (Preferred): Initial Liquidity Pool. If sufficient capital is available at launch, a small initial SUDX/USDC liquidity pool will be created and locked to ensure a stable trading environment from day one.

Scenario B (Contingency): Direct to Market. If sufficient capital for a pool is not available, the token will be launched directly. In this case, liquidity will be built organically by the community over time. This is a transparent approach to ensure the project can launch regardless of initial funding.

Team & Founders

15% (420M SUDX)
To reward the founding team, subject to a long-term vesting schedule to ensure commitment.

Marketing & Partnerships

10% (280M SUDX)
To drive adoption, form strategic partnerships, and increase the project's visibility.

Strategic Treasury

5% (140M SUDX)
A reserve for future opportunities and expenses, governed by the DAO.

Trust & Safety

To guarantee investor security, team tokens are vested over a 3-year period. The entire initial liquidity pool will be locked for a minimum of 6 months, preventing rug pulls and demonstrating our long-term commitment.
