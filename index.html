The Code is Free, but its Developers Are Not.

Free and open-source software runs the world. From the servers that power the internet to the Linux ecosystem that champions freedom, it's all built by a global community. However, its creators—the developers, maintainers, and designers—often rely on unstable donations, volunteer work, and fragmented funding.

Community gratitude exists, but it rarely converts into fair and sustainable financial support. SUDX was born to fix this, creating a direct and transparent bridge between the open-source ethos and the funding power of Web3.

Turning Gratitude into Action.

In the Linux terminal, sudo is the command that grants elevated privileges to execute critical tasks. It embodies trust, power, and responsibility. Our currency, SUDX, is born from this philosophy to become the native token of the open-source ecosystem. The X stands for extension, experimentation, and the future we will build together.

Tokenomics: A Transparent Blueprint

Our tokenomics are designed for sustainable growth and community empowerment. Below is a transparent breakdown of our total supply of 2,800,000,000 SUDX and the strategy behind each allocation.

Ecosystem Fund & Rewards 45%

Held in a secure Safe and transferred to the DAO treasury via community vote. This protects funds while empowering the community to finance open-source development.

Liquidity Pool ~25%

Reserved for market health. An initial pool is locked, and the rest will be added strategically to deepen liquidity as the project grows, governed by the DAO.

Team & Founders 15%

Locked in a 3-year vesting contract with a 12-month cliff. This aligns the team's incentives with the long-term success of the project. Verifiable on-chain.

Marketing & Partnerships 10%

Dedicated funds to build awareness, forge strategic partnerships, and accelerate adoption.

Strategic Treasury 5%

A reserve for unforeseen opportunities and future operational costs, ultimately controlled by the DAO.
